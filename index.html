Jaedong Lee

Holiday Robotics · jaedong.lee [at] holiday-robotics.com

I am a senior research engineer at Holiday Robotics , focusing on whole-body manipulation with learning-based approaches and sim-to-real deployment. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from Seoul National University, advised by Jehee Lee and Jungdam Won. During my Ph.D., I studied the generation of natural human motion using simulation-based reinforcement learning, with a focus on multi-agent navigation, locomotion, and human–exoskeleton interaction.
